# Aperture Science — Testing & Safety Information

Welcome, test subject. Your participation advances science.

## General Guidelines
- All testing takes place within the Enrichment Center under GLaDOS supervision.
- Test chambers may contain Aperture Science Material Emancipation Grills, High Energy
  Pellets, and Unstationary Scaffolds. Exercise caution.
- The cake, and the consumption of the cake, is offered upon successful completion of
  all test chambers. The cake is a real and moist delight.

## Hazards
- Toxic liquid, deadly lasers, and Sentry Turrets may be present in advanced chambers.
- Do not touch the operational end of the Aperture Science Sentry Turret.
- In the event of a Companion Cube attachment, euthanization protocols may apply.

## Emergency Information
- There is no emergency exit. Please remain calm and continue testing.
- Should you experience temporal displacement, mild hallucinations, or a sudden
  philosophical crisis, these are normal side effects of scientific progress.

## Contact
For inquiries, address all correspondence to the Aperture Science Enrichment Center.
Responses are not guaranteed. Neither is your continued participation.
