# Aperture Science — Products

## Handheld Portal Device (ASHPD)
The portal gun creates two linked portals on flat surfaces, allowing instantaneous
travel and the conservation of momentum ("speedy thing goes in, speedy thing comes out").

## Weighted Companion Cube
A storage cube issued to test subjects as a morale aid. The Enrichment Center reminds
you that the Weighted Companion Cube will never threaten to stab you and, in fact,
cannot speak.

## Aerial Faith Plates
Spring-loaded plates that launch test subjects across large gaps as part of advanced
testing courses.

## Propulsion and Repulsion Gels
Experimental moon-rock-derived gels. Propulsion gel (orange) increases movement speed;
repulsion gel (blue) causes objects to bounce.

## Combustible Lemons
A patented Cave Johnson initiative. When life gives you lemons, do not make lemonade —
make life take the lemons back.

## GLaDOS
The Genetic Lifeform and Disk Operating System, an artificial intelligence that
administers the Enrichment Center and supervises all testing.
